About the Quantum Narrative School

Source: https://www.q-n-s.com/?pages_1/

The Quantum Narrative School is an interdisciplinary theoretical system founded by Ma Nanjie. Focusing on exploring the logic of the Creator (the builder of the universe) to construct all beings and the relationship between beings, we are committed to constructing a corresponding quantitative interpretation framework. This school breaks through the boundaries of traditional disciplines, integrates multidisciplinary knowledge such as mathematics, quantum physics, molecular biology, neuroscience, philosophy, psychology, semiotics, art, etc., excavates the internal connections and deep logic of all beings and their existential relationships, and provides a new perspective and way for understanding and exploring the Creator (the builder of the universe) to construct all beings and their relationships through unique theories and methods. Ma Nanjie, the founder and builder of the Quantum Narrative School, a member of the Shaanxi Artists Association and a young artist, also known as GREENDRAGON, graduated from the Academy of Fine Arts in Venice, Italy, majoring in painting in his early years, under the tutelage of Professor Igor Lecic. Over the years, the main subjects have been fully scored, and have been praised by deans, professors and industry insiders all year round. He is also the author of the core definitions of the quantum narrative school: tactile sovereignty, entropy democracy, synaptic parliament, screen shaman, synaptic cybernetics, topological time, mathematical neural dictionary, quantum narrative matrix, and cellular molecular metabolism politics.

量子叙事学派是一个跨学科理论体系，创建者马楠杰。以探索造物主（宇宙构建者）构建一切存在者及存在者关系的逻辑为核心，致力于构建相应的量化诠释框架。该学派突破传统学科界限，融合数学、量子物理学、分子生物学、神经科学、哲学、心理学、符号学、艺术等多学科知识，挖掘一切存在者及其存在关系的内在联系及深层逻辑，通过独特的理论和方法，为理解探索造物主（宇宙构建者）构建一切存在者及其关系提供全新的视角与途径。量子叙事学派创始人、构建者陕西省美术家协会会员、青年艺术家马楠杰别名（青龙），早年毕业于意大利威尼斯美术学院绘画专业，师从Igor Lecic教授。历年主科全满分常年获得院长教授及业内人士好评。也是量子叙事学派核心定义：触觉主权、熵民主、突触议会、屏幕萨满、突触控制论、拓扑时间、数学神经词典、量子叙事矩阵、细胞分子代谢政治学的作者。
