Introduction to the founder of the quantum narrative school

Ma Nanjie, the founder and builder of the Quantum Narrative School, a member of the Shaanxi Artists Association and a young artist, also known as GREENDRAGON, graduated from the Academy of Fine Arts in Venice, Italy, majoring in painting in his early years, under the tutelage of Professor Igor Lecic. Over the years, the main subjects have been fully scored, and have been praised by deans, professors and industry insiders all year round. He is also the author of the core definitions of the quantum narrative school: tactile sovereignty, entropy democracy, synaptic parliament, screen shaman, synaptic cybernetics, topological time, mathematical neural dictionary, quantum narrative matrix, and cellular molecular metabolism politics.

Introduction to the Quantum Narrative School

Founded on February 25, 2025 in Xi'an, Shaanxi, China, the Quantum Narrative School is a cutting-edge interdisciplinary theoretical system founded by Ma Nanjie, a young painter who is a member of the Shaanxi Artists Association. Focusing on exploring the logic of the Creator (the builder of the universe) to construct all beings and the relationship between beings, we are committed to constructing a corresponding quantitative interpretation framework. Breaking through the boundaries of traditional disciplines, this school integrates multidisciplinary knowledge such as mathematics, quantum physics, molecular biology, neuroscience, philosophy, psychology, semiotics, art, etc., deeply excavates the internal connections and deep logic of all beings and their existential relationships, and provides a new perspective and way for understanding and exploring the Creator (the builder of the universe) to construct all beings and their relationships through unique theories and methods.

Differences from the existing quantum narrative core:

The quantum narrative school integrates multidisciplinary exploration of beings and their relationships, and there are many differences from other quantum narrative-related content:

  1. The purpose of theoretical construction: This school of thought focuses on exploring the logic of the Creator's construction of all beings and their relationships, and is committed to constructing a quantitative interpretive framework to explain the logic of the world's composition from the root. Other quantum narratives may only focus on the innovation of the narrative itself, or focus on the simple application of quantum theory in the narrative, lacking in-depth exploration of the essence of existence.
  1. Depth of discipline integration: Integrate multidisciplinary knowledge such as mathematics and quantum physics to form a unique theoretical system, such as quantum narrative matrix, synaptic cybernetics, etc.; Some other quantum narratives only draw on quantum concepts at a superficial level, and do not truly integrate multidisciplinary construction system theory.
  1. Narrative model innovation: Models such as quantum narrative matrix are proposed to break through the traditional linear narrative and encode story elements with tensor networks in high-dimensional Hilbert space. Other quantum narratives may not have jumped out of the traditional narrative framework and made full use of quantum characteristics to construct new narrative structures.
  1. Application field expansion: It has been applied in many fields such as molecular metabolism politics, artistic creation analysis, and human-computer interaction; The application of other quantum narratives may be limited to literary narrative analysis, and there is a lack of innovative applications in multiple fields.
  1. Application of uncertainty: Deeply integrate the quantum uncertainty principle into theory, such as narrative development, political analysis, etc.; Other quantum narratives mention uncertainty, but they may not be integrated into all theoretical links and application scenarios.

Catalogue of original definitions of the quantum narrative school

o 1 熵民主（Entropic Democracy）

It refers to the theoretical framework of constructing a consensus evolution model of group decision-making based on the information entropy weight allocation mechanism. Its innovation is reflected in the systematic application of information theory tools to social decision-making analysis, which provides a new paradigm for the quantitative research of democratic science.

o 2 拓扑时间（Topological Time）

It refers to a theoretical framework based on algebraic topology theory that models time manifolds as high-dimensional causal structures. Its innovation is reflected in the systematic application of mathematical topology tools to historical narrative analysis, which provides a new paradigm for scientific research in the time dimension.

o 3 细胞分子代谢政治学（Molecular Metabolic Politics）

It refers to the construction of a theoretical framework for the analysis of political power structure based on the regulatory mechanism of cellular metabolic network. Its innovation is reflected in the systematic application of systems biology tools to political science research, providing a new paradigm for the quantitative analysis of power dynamics. It is also an attempt to break through the boundaries of existing micropolitics neuropolitics.

4 屏幕萨满(Screen Shaman)

It refers to the theoretical framework of dynamic matching of digital interface information flow and human cognitive features through three mechanisms: neuroplasticity adaptation, cultural symbol topology mapping and attention manifold optimization. Its core innovation lies in the systematic application of cultural neuroscience principles to human-computer interaction design, breaking through the static paradigm of traditional UI.

o 5 触觉主权（Tactile Sovereignty）

It refers to the fact that in the context of digital survival, individuals maintain their perceptual control over tactile interaction through the neurotactile mapping mechanism, and construct a theoretical system of interaction ethics based on tactile political topology. Its innovation is reflected in the integration of neuroscientific principles with political philosophical paradigms to establish a tactile dimension of digital human rights framework.

o 6 突触控制论 （Synaptic Cybernetics）

It is a complex system regulation theory based on the "quantum tunneling hypothesis", and its core framework includes the following original elements:

  1. "Heterogeneous recursive topology": Mapping of biological synaptic plasticity to control signals by bidirectional time-delayed differential equations
  1. Calcium oscillation decision model: based on synaptic cleft Ca²⁺ phase synchronization, a dynamic threshold response mechanism is established
  1. "Directional control of entropy flow": The path constraint of system evolution is realized by using the information entropy gradient field

o 7 Quantum Narrative Matrix is a narrative topology model based on quantum computing logic, which encodes story elements into tensor networks in high-dimensional Hilbert space, and realizes the emergence of dynamic narrative paths through "narrative operator entanglement" and "context superposition state collapse".

The Mathematical Neural Dictionary is based on modern mathematical and physical tools such as non-commutative geometry, higher-order category theory and quantum field theory, and realizes the axiomatic terminology system of mathematical neuroscience by establishing a strict correspondence framework between mathematical concepts and neuroscience.
