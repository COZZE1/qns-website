This article provides a concise interpretation of the core logic of the Quantum Narrative School from the perspective of systematic quantitative humanism.

At a time when humanities research largely relies on qualitative descriptions and "language barriers" exist between disciplines, the research of the Quantum Narrative School aims to build a bridge connecting science and humanities. By constructing a large-scale interdisciplinary quantitative framework, it lays the foundation for the new paradigm of "systematic quantitative humanism." This research not only defines unique concepts suitable for interdisciplinary readers but also clearly sorts out the theoretical application paths and core values. The following is a systematic presentation of the core logic of this theoretical system.

I. Research Positioning: An Interdisciplinary Exploration Laying the Foundation for "Quantitative Humanism" This research is a key link in the continuous construction of a large-scale, interdisciplinary quantitative framework. Its core goal is to break the limitation of "qualitative-dominance" in traditional humanities research and establish a set of "common language" that can connect multiple fields such as art, politics, biology, and cognition.

This theoretical framework is complex and grand, with a clear hierarchical structure in content classification:

  • Theoretical Classification: Scientific empirical research, rational metaphor, and philosophical speculation. This ensures that each part is rigorous while retaining room for exploration.
  • Formula Classification: Scientifically computable formulas, rational metaphorical formulas, and formulas to be adjusted. These cover full-scale analysis from the micro-level (e.g., neurometabolic parameters) to the macro-level (e.g., social power flow).
  • Optimization Logic: To enhance the accuracy, scientificity, and rigor of the system, continuous testing and iterative optimization are required. If the framework is verified through empirical research, it can generate local research results in multiple fields.

II. Core of Interdisciplinary Integration: The "Common Language" and the Four Pillar Systems

The interdisciplinary integration of the Quantum Narrative School is not a simple combination of concepts. Instead, it takes "quantitative tools" as the "common language" and constructs four pillar systems to form a complete theoretical support system.

  1. First Pillar: Basic Support Platform – The "Infrastructure" of the System

As the "hardware foundation" for the implementation of the theory, it provides operational support for all tools:

  • Data Flow: The "fuel" of the system, covering two types of core data:

① Empirical data related to the audience (e.g., visual feedback of art audiences, behavioral data of policy recipients); ② AI simulation data (see AI-Assisted Coding Report and Triple Trinity Closed-Loop Optimization Test V1.1 for details), ensuring that the theory is not divorced from practical data support.

  • Quantum Computing: The "acceleration engine" of the system. It solves the computing bottleneck of high-dimensional data (e.g., high-dimensional narrative coding, multivariate metabolic analysis) and improves the efficiency of quantitative analysis.
  1. Second Pillar: Core Theoretical Tools – "Methodological Weapons" for Solving Specific Problems

It provides 6 types of applicable quantitative tools to address pain points in different fields, covering the entire process from coding to application:

  1. Quantum Narrative Matrix: A core coding tool that can convert all existing entities (e.g., cultural symbols, policy texts, biological metabolic networks) into mathematical structures. Although full verification is difficult with current technology, complete coding will be realized in the future with the development of quantum computing and AI (see the case of "Topological Time and Biological Metabolic Coding" in The Theory of the Quantum Narrative School for details).
  2. Mathematical Neural Dictionary: The "Rosetta Stone" for breaking the "disciplinary language barrier". It establishes connections between concepts such as "synaptic plasticity" in neuroscience, "free will" in philosophy, "creative inspiration" in art, and "cellular metabolic regulation" in biology through mathematical models (see the "Axiomatic System of the Mathematical Neural Dictionary" in The Theory of the Quantum Narrative School and The Trinity Personal Closed-Loop System in Quantitative Cross-Media Narrative Research: Integration of Neural Symbolic Translation and Quantitative Formulas for details).
  3. Entropy Democracy Model: A core tool for group decision-making. It quantifies the evolution of consensus through "information entropy" and solves the "voting paradox" in traditional democratic theory.
  4. Quantitative Formula System: Analytical tools covering from micro to macro levels, such as "element distribution entropy", "brushwork dynamics formula", and "cellular metabolic flux analogy formula" (see The Triple Trinity Personal Closed-Loop System and all related papers for details).
  5. Cellular and Molecular Metabolic Political Tools: By analogy between "cellular metabolic flow" and "social power flow", it realizes the quantitative analysis of political decision-making (see The Theory of the Quantum Narrative School for details).
  6. Screen Shaman and Tactile Sovereignty Tools: Focusing on human-computer interaction in the digital age, they address the issue of "cognitive freedom and biological perception protection" (see The Theory of the Quantum Narrative School for details).
  1. Third Pillar: Independent Philosophical Cornerstone – The Quantum-Neural-Biological Emergence Model of Free Will

As the "ideological core" of the theory, it provides philosophical support for quantitative analysis. Its core is to explain the generation mechanism of free will through the three-layer coupling of "quantum-neural-biology" (see An Ontological Exploration of Free Will under the Framework of the Quantum Narrative School for details), preventing quantitative tools from falling into "data-onlyism".

4. Fourth Pillar: Cross-Cultural Adaptation Mechanism

It ensures the universality of the theory. By adjusting core parameters (e.g., when analyzing the "White Tiger symbol", East Asian culture focuses on "inheritance", while Western culture emphasizes "individual interpretation"), the quantitative tools are adapted to different cultural scenarios (see The Theory of the Quantum Narrative School for details).

III. Ultimate Form of the Theory: A Complete Closed Loop of "Input-Processing-Output"

The theory of the Quantum Narrative School is not a static framework but a self-optimizing "operating system" with a clear and closed-loop workflow:

  1. Input: Data flow (e.g., fMRI neural data, cellular metabolic parameters, cultural symbol data);
  2. Coding and Translation: Complete high-dimensional coding through the "Quantum Narrative Matrix", and then convert it into interdisciplinary parameters (e.g., converting "metabolic flow tensor" into "power flow weight") via the "Mathematical Neural Dictionary";
  3. Computation: Distribute parameters to tools such as "Entropy Democracy", "Art Quantification", and "Cellular Politics" for targeted analysis;
  4. Implementation and Feedback: Inject computational results into the "Trinity Closed-Loop" to guide practices such as artistic creation, policy-making, interface optimization, and biological metabolic analysis;
  5. Empowerment and Adaptation: "Quantum computing" accelerates computing power throughout the process, and the "cross-cultural adaptation mechanism" ensures that results are adapted to different scenarios. Finally, a complete chain of "input-processing-output-feedback" is formed.

Among them, the "Triple Trinity Super Closed-Loop" is the key to the implementation of the theory. It covers three dimensions: "theory-formula-practice", "digital-text-art language", and "creative subject-cognitive object-communication carrier". Each dimension realizes "three-way synergy" and integrates biological and cross-cultural elements to form a self-optimizing system (see "Closed-Loop Logic and Multi-Domain Cases" in The Trinity Personal Closed-Loop System in Quantitative Cross-Media Narrative Research: Integration of Neural Symbolic Translation and Quantitative Formulas for details).

IV. Application Value of the Theory: Insights from Scientific Empiricism to Philosophical Speculation

  1. Scientific Empiricism and Rational Metaphor Levels: Solving Problems in Specific Fields

When focusing on scientific empiricism and rational metaphor, the theory has broad and applicable application value:

  • Political Analysis: Quantitatively analyze the political decision-making tendencies (e.g., appointment of core officials, war risk preference) of decision-makers through their molecular structures and cellular metabolic data;
  • Art Research: Break through the vague descriptions of traditional criticism (e.g., "highly dynamic colors") and quantify the visual characteristics of artworks using "element distribution entropy" and "brushwork dynamics formula";

If extended to philosophical speculation, the theory may provide a new perspective for understanding humanity’s ultimate positioning:

  • Core Hypothesis: Humans and artificial intelligence may both be "advanced robots", part of the cosmic metabolism constrained by cosmic laws, with limited boundaries in creativity (this hypothesis requires further scientific verification);
